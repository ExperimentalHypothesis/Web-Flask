This is so far a site in an experimental state


Due the the fact that I have been involved in dicretionary derivative trading on US future markets for couple of years and lately started to build automatic trading systems, I realised the need for a tool to monitor the AOS porfolio.

The primary goal of this website is thus to serve as a dasboard for some of the systems that are curently launched on CME or CBOT. I hope I will get a better insight based upon the performance, stats and probabilities that are plotted graphically on this web.

All the trading systems are written in C++. The website is build in Python. For database I use MySQL 8.0.
